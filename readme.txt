== Description ==

<strong>Add an eye-catching chat/floating bubble that follows your customers anywhere throughout their visits to your website.</strong>

(Tiếng Việt: <a href="https://vi.wordpress.org/plugins/chat-bubble/">https://vi.wordpress.org/plugins/chat-bubble/</a>)

= The bubble serves many purposes, depends on what you want it to be: =
* Display your information: business hours, website URL, Google map, etc.
* Call me back (+ Scheduler)
* Click to call/email/SMS
* Click to chat: Tawk.to, Zalo, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Skype, LINE, Telegram, Viber, and others

= Why should you use this chat bubble? =
1. Keep track of your visitors' click actions: Smart way to know what your potential customers is interested in. 
2. Boost interactions and engagements on your website 
3. Easy to use: No coding needed on your part.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `chat-bubble` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory via FTP
2. Alternatively, upload the `chat-bubble.zip` file to the 'Plugins->Add New' page in your WordPress admin
area
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

1, 2, 3: You're done!
